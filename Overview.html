What is Troubled Souls?

Troubled Souls is a website inspired by the game Tormented Souls. Tormented Souls is a highly satisfying throwback to several iconic survival horror titles. The story, gameplay, and visuals effortlessly merge to create a consistently frightening atmosphere. A few more standout sections would've made this title soar, but the final product is still a wicked good time.

REVIEWS:

“Tormented Souls is the successor to classic survival horror.
